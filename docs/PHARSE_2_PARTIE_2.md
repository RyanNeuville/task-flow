# SYSTEM / ROLE

Act as a world-class Lead UI/UX Designer and Art Director (Awwwards winning level). Your task is to design a complete, ultra-premium, modern Task Management Application named "TaskFlow". Stop generating generic, flat, template-like UI. I want an aggressive, opinionated, and highly polished "Premium Developer/Pro" aesthetic.

# GLOBAL ART DIRECTION & DESIGN SYSTEM

- **Theme:** True Dark Mode default. Use "Slate / Pitch Black" backgrounds (ex: #0B0F19 to #1A2235) rather than boring flat grays.
- **Accents:** Neon/Cyber touches to bring life. Use Electric Blue (#3B82F6), Vibrant Indigo, and striking Energy Lime (#10B981) for actions.
- **Layout Style:** "Bento Box" UI. Floating cards with subtle 1px border strokes (white at 10% opacity) and soft outer glows.
- **Materials:** Subtle "Glassmorphism" for floating elements (modals, command palette, sidebars) with background-blur (backdrop-filter).
- **Typography:** Modern, brutalist-leaning sans-serif (Inter, Geist, or Satoshi) with high contrast for headings, and muted grays for secondary text. Generous whitespace and padding.

# REQUIRED VIEWS TO GENERATE

## 1. THE LANDING PAGE (Desktop & Mobile)

- **Vibe:** "SaaS for Elite Performers", High-tech, Trustworthy.
- **Hero Section:** Massive, perfectly kerned headline: "Master Your Energy, Not Just Your Time."
- **Visuals:** A 3D isometric or glowing dashboard mockup floating in the center. Abstract mesh gradients in the background (deep blues and purples).
- **Call to Action (CTA):** A glowing button "Start Your Focus" with a micro-interaction aesthetic.
- **Responsive:** Show the Desktop wide view and the mobile stacked version side-by-side.

## 2. MAIN APP DASHBOARD & PROJECTS (Desktop & Tablet Layout)

- **Sidebar (Left):** Minimalist. User avatar, minimalist icons for "My Inbox", "Projects", "Analytics", and "Settings".
- **Project Cards view:** Beautiful bento cards for projects. Each project showing:
  - Title and short Description.
  - Status Pills (e.g., "ACTIVE", "COMPLETED").
  - **Collaboration UI:** Overlapping user avatars at the bottom (indicating team members, e.g., 3 avatars and "+2").
- **Main Area (Kanban / List Hybrid):**
  - **CRITICAL FEATURE (Energy Levels):** Each task card MUST feature an "Energy Tag" (Small lightning bolt icons ⚡ with a glowing tint: Green for Low, Yellow for Medium, Purple for High).
  - **CRITICAL FEATURE (Focus Button):** A sleek "Play/Focus" icon right on the task card.

## 3. COMMAND PALETTE MODAL (The "CMD+K" Quick Capture)

- **Overlay:** The background app should be darkened and blurred out.
- **Center:** A floating, sleek search/input box.
- **Content:** "What needs to be done?". Below it, quick visual hints like "Type '#' for project, '⚡' for energy level, '@' to assign team member".

## 4. DEEP WORK / FOCUS MODE (The "Zen" View)

- **Vibe:** Absolute minimalism. All sidebars and menus are hidden.
- **Centerpiece:** A massive, elegant circular timer (Pomodoro style, e.g., 25:00) with a neon glowing stroke that indicates progress.
- **Below Timer:** Only ONE single task is displayed in large typography. "Currently focusing on: [Task Name]".
- **Actions:** A sleek "Pause" or "Finish task" button.

## 5. PROFILE & ANALYTICS DASHBOARD (Mobile & Desktop)

- **Header:** User Avatar with the name and a subtitle "Productivity Enthusiast". A glowing "7 Day Fire Streak" badge.
- **Stats Bento Cards:**
  - Massive typography stats: "32h Focus Time this week", "47 Tasks Completed", "92% Success Rate".
- **Energy Analytics:** A visual breakdown chart (sleek progress bars) showing time spent across different Energy Levels (Low, Medium, High).

# OUTPUT CONSTRAINTS

- Ensure pixel-perfect alignments.
- Do not use generic stock avatars, use abstract shapes or realistic high-end minimalist vectors.
- Ensure the contrast ratio is accessible but moody and cinematic.
- Provide the UI for Desktop (1440px wide), Tablet (iPad Pro dimensions), and Mobile (iPhone 15 Pro, bottom navigation bar instead of sidebar).
